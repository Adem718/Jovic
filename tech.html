Academics Program

We believe that a rigorous academic program combined with a supportive social-emotional curriculum leads to scholar success.
TBCSC's academic program reflects the practices that are associated with high-performing urban schools: more time on task;
standards-based instruction; research-based curricula; regular assessments; nightly homework; and horizon-broadening enrichment
opportunities.
