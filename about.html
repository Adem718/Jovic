Our Mission



The Mission of The Bronx Charter School for Children is to empower our children to achieve their greatest potential both as students and as
members of their communities.

Core Values

Fostering Academic Excellence



By supporting skilled and dedicated teachers
who maintain high academic standards,
motivate our
students and collaborate with our families

Developing Critical Thinkers



By encouraging intellectual curiosity and
rewarding independent and creative problem
solvers

Nurturing the Whole Child



By creating engaged learners, encouraging
honesty and guiding social and emotional
development

Ensuring a Safe Environment



By promoting mutual respect in our diverse
community of students, teachers and families
